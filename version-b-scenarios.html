Creative Value Assessment

Scenario-Based Format

How this works: Each question presents a realistic scenario. Pick the response that best describes how your organisation would actually behave — not how you wish it would. Each option reflects a different level of creative maturity.
Topic 1 — Evaluation Priorities

A team member proposes a bold new campaign concept that's unlike anything your organisation has done before. What happens next?

Topic 2 — Decision-Making Authority

Two departments disagree on the creative direction for a high-profile project. How is the conflict resolved?

Topic 3 — Measuring Success

A major creative project has just launched. Six weeks later, how does your organisation decide whether it was a success?

Topic 4 — Handling Rejection

A creative lead has just had their concept rejected for the third time this quarter. What's the atmosphere like?

Topic 5 — Creative Risk Appetite

Your organisation has the opportunity to sponsor a culturally relevant but edgy creative collaboration. What's the likely outcome?

Please answer all 5 scenarios before submitting.
