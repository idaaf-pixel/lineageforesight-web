Creative Value Assessment

Binary + Branching Format

How this works: Answer each Yes/No question honestly. A follow-up question will appear based on your answer to dig deeper into your organisation's reality.
Topic 1 — Evaluation Priorities

Does your organisation have explicit criteria for evaluating creative ideas?

Follow-up

How consistently are those criteria actually applied?

Follow-up

What tends to drive the decision instead?

Topic 2 — Decision-Making Authority

Is there a clearly designated person or group who makes final creative decisions?

Follow-up

Does the creative team feel that decision-maker understands creative value?

Follow-up

What usually happens when there's no clear decision-maker?

Topic 3 — Measuring Success

Does your organisation formally review the success of creative projects after they launch?

Follow-up

Do those reviews capture creative quality, or just performance metrics?

Follow-up

Why not?

Topic 4 — Handling Rejection

When a creative idea is rejected, is the reasoning clearly communicated to the person who proposed it?

Follow-up

Does that feedback help the creator improve their next pitch?

Follow-up

What effect does that have on the creative team?

Topic 5 — Creative Risk Appetite

Has your organisation taken a genuine creative risk in the last 12 months?

Follow-up

How did the organisation respond to the outcome?

Follow-up

What's the main barrier to creative risk-taking?
